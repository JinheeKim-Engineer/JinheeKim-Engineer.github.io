## Language-Guided Outdoor Navigation for Quadruped Robots
**Jul. 2025 – Sept. 2025 | Graduate Researcher**

![Project Image](/assets/이미지파일이름.jpg)

### Overview
* Generating routes from natural language instructions that reflect both destinations and user-specified travel conditions.
* Leveraging OSM data to plan paths without requiring prior map or database construction.
* Enabling perception-based updates to navigation graphs for dynamic environments.
