## Autonomous Driving System Development
**Mar. 2023 – Dec. 2023 | Team Member**

![Project Image](/assets/frozen.jpg)

### Overview
* Contributed to the development and testing of a small-scale autonomous vehicle platform.
* Implemented lane recognition (LiDAR), obstacle avoidance (sensor fusion), parking (Hybrid A*), and narrow road driving (RRT).
* Built for the 2023 Self-Driving Software Advanced Competition and successfully completed all mission tasks during the live event.
